About Me

Professional Biography

Habiby (SMAJ) is a visionary entrepreneur and CEO who founded SMAJ with a mission to revolutionize digital ecosystems through innovative technology and cryptocurrency solutions. With a background in business development and tech innovation, Habiby has successfully launched multiple ventures that bridge traditional commerce with cutting-edge blockchain technology.

Founder Story

The journey of SMAJ began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create digital platforms that empower users worldwide. Starting from humble beginnings, Habiby identified the potential of Pi Network and cryptocurrency to transform e-commerce and digital services. This led to the creation of SMAJ STORE and SMAJ PI HUB, pioneering platforms that make crypto commerce accessible to everyone.

Vision & Leadership

Under Habiby's leadership, SMAJ continues to push boundaries in the crypto and tech space. The philosophy centers on building sustainable, user-centric platforms that combine innovation with real-world utility. SMAJ's approach focuses on creating value for users while maintaining the highest standards of security, transparency, and innovation.
